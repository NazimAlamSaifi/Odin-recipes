Burger

This juicy, flame-grilled beef burger is packed with flavor and layered with melted cheddar cheese, fresh lettuce,
sliced tomatoes, and crunchy pickles. It's served on a perfectly toasted sesame seed bun and topped with a tangy homemade burger
sauce. A delicious blend of savory beef, creamy cheese, and crisp veggies, creating the perfect bite every time.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat for juiciness)
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 sesame seed burger buns
  • 4 leaves of fresh lettuce
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 8 pickle slices
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or butter (for cooking)

For the Sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon pickle relish (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the patties:
    • Divide the ground beef into four equal portions and gently shape each portion into a patty, about 1 inch thick. Make a small indentation in the center of each patty to help it cook evenly.
    • Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties:
    • Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Add a little olive oil or butter for flavor.
    • Place the patties on the hot skillet or grill, cooking for about 4-5 minutes per side (or until your desired doneness).
    • During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheddar cheese to each patty and cover the pan to let the cheese melt.
  3. Toast the buns:
    • While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns in a separate pan or on the grill until golden brown.
  4. Make the sauce:
    • In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, pickle relish (if using), lemon juice, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Assemble the burgers:
    • Spread the sauce on the bottom halves of the toasted buns.
    • Add a leaf of lettuce, a slice of tomato, a few onion slices (if using), and pickle slices to each bun.
    • Place the cheeseburger patties on top, then cover with the top halves of the buns.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve your burgers hot with fries, onion rings, or your favorite sides.
