lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (preferably San Marzano tomatoes)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • A pinch of crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Heat your oven to its highest setting (usually around 250°C or 500°F). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  2. Prepare the dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface until it reaches your desired thickness.
  3. Assemble the pizza: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Place slices of fresh mozzarella evenly over the sauce.
  4. Season: Drizzle with olive oil, then sprinkle oregano, a bit of salt, and (if you like) crushed red pepper flakes over the top.
  5. Bake: Transfer the pizza to the oven (or pizza stone) and bake for about 8-10 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. Finish with basil: Remove the pizza from the oven and immediately add fresh basil leaves. Drizzle a bit more olive oil if desired and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
  7. Serve: Slice and serve hot!
